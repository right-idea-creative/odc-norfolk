Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Is same-day service available?+
Same-day service is available subject to technician availability. Call 757-363-0330 and we'll give you an accurate arrival window based on the current schedule.
What does "$0 service call" mean?+
When you approve and complete a repair, the service call fee is waived. Your technician provides a written estimate before any work begins. If you choose not to proceed, a standard service call fee applies.
What is a non-commission technician?+
Most garage door companies pay techs on commission — they earn more when your repair costs more. Ours are salaried employees. Their incentive is accuracy, not upsell.
Is a broken spring dangerous?+
Yes. Torsion springs are under significant tension. Don't attempt to open the door or repair the spring yourself. Leave the door in place and call us.
Do you service all brands?+
Yes. We service all major brands — Clopay, Amarr, Wayne Dalton, LiftMaster, Genie, Chamberlain, and more. Most common parts are stocked on our trucks.
